Fortress Manager Mini

A minigame about managing a Team Fortress 2 team in an imaginary league.

Practice


Choose a practice mode

  • Dexterity: Players hone their sense & muscle memory playing a series of practice gamemodes.
  • Review: The team goes over mistakes done in previous matches and comes up with new tactics.
  • Coaching: You hire a professional coach to improve your team's approach to future games.
